Description
This module allows pay with Polish service for installment loans as Drupal Commerce payment method.

Module creates
1. Block "How to buy".
2. Buttons to calculate placed under shopping cart overview and one in product display page.
3. Payment method "eRaty".

Installation
1. Unpack and move directory "commerce_eraty" to your modules directory.
2. Enable it in the modules list of your site.
4. Move block "How to buy with eRaty?" in region of your choice.
5. Enable and configure eRaty payment method at "admin/commerce/config/payment-methods".
